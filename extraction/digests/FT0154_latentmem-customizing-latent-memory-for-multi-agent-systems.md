## Key Extracted Facts

- Architecture status: `arch_experience_bank`.
- Storage: experience bank of raw multi-agent trajectories plus learned role-aware latent memories generated by a memory composer.
- Evaluation: TriviaQA, KodCode, StrategyQA, PopQA, BigCodeBench, and PDDL across AutoGen, MacNet, CAMEL, and DyLAN.
- Main result: LatentMem reports up to 19.36% gain over vanilla settings and consistently outperforms memory baselines.
- Performance versus baseline: average scores improve from 52.86 to 61.82 on AutoGen, 53.38 to 61.00 on MacNet, 53.37 to 60.04 on CAMEL, and 52.80 to 60.06 on DyLAN.
